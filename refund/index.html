Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Overview

This policy outlines the conditions under which orders placed through ATS Gen Pro may be cancelled or refunded.

2. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 0 days of purchase. After this window, cancellations are not accepted.

3. Refund Process

Refunds are processed only after eligibility verification. To request a refund:

  • Contact our support team through the Google Play Store listing
  • Provide your order details and reason for the refund request
  • Allow up to 7–14 business days for review and processing

Approved refunds will be credited to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Personalized items — products or services customized to your specifications
  • Sale or promotional items — purchases made during special discount periods
  • Restricted items — certain digital goods or services as marked at the time of purchase

5. Google Play Purchases

For in-app purchases made through Google Play, refund requests may also be submitted directly through the Google Play refund process, subject to Google's refund policies.

6. Contact

For refund inquiries, please contact us via the support channel listed on our Google Play Store page.
